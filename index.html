About us

The [SEA] Black Marine Corps constitutes a player division deeply committed to the game "Navy Simulator."
We're more than just a group; we're a community of friends united by shared enjoyment and the celebration of achievements.
Our primary focus is to ready you for raids, enhancing both your skills and understanding of battle tactics!

Additionally, we boast two subdivisions.


Special Tactics Group


The first is the STG, which stands for Special Tactics Group.

The Special Tactics Group is a rigorous community emphasizing aim skills, TPS and FPS expertise, and comprehensive training in tactical knowledge.
STG gives top priority to every raid, so if you aspire to join us in STG, train diligently and strive to succeed in our STG tryout!





Airborne Squadron


The second subdivision is AS, which stands for Airborne Squadron.

Airborne Squadron is a regular group centered around flying skills and anti-air skills.
Here, you'll learn how to eliminate opponents effortlessly.
It's highly recommended for raids, supporting STG in navigating their way through challenges.
So if you are aspire to join us in AS, train diligently and strive to succeed in our AS tryout!
